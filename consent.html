Informed Consent Agreement

Project Title: Implicit Social Cognition on the Internet

This study examines opinions and judgments related to attitudes, preferences and beliefs. You will read some instructions, complete a task where you will categorize words or pictures into groups as fast as possible. At the end, you will receive feedback about your performance and some background information on purpose of the research. The study will require about 12 minutes in total.

This study may help us understand how people think, and it may provide an educational, engaging experience. Project Implicit's standard privacy policy applies to this study.

Participation in this study is voluntary, and you may end your participation at any time by closing the study window. Contact Calvin Lai (ckl5ae@virginia.edu) if you have any questions about the study.
